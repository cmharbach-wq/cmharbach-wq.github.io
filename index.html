About Me

A little about who I am.

I'm the kind of person who'd rather ask you a real question than make small talk. That's probably why sociology pulled me in.

Right now I run social media for Sora Fit, a health and fitness startup, where I plan and shoot content for Instagram and TikTok. Before that I interned at Chatbooks, beta-testing a new journaling app and flagging the rough edges before real users hit them.

Outside school and work, I'm usually thinking about mental health — specifically, how to make support feel less intimidating for people my age.
